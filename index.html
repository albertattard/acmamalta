Stainless Steel Works

Our workshop specialises in the design and fabrication of high-quality custom stainless steel works, catering to both domestic and commercial projects. With expertise spanning everything from elegant stainless steel railings for homes and businesses to bespoke fittings for marine applications, we combine craftsmanship, precision, and durability in every piece we produce. Whether working on a small, intricate detail or a large-scale installation, we pride ourselves on delivering exceptional results tailored to each client’s exact requirements. Our experience with boat fittings and other specialised projects ensures a versatile service backed by technical skill and a commitment to excellence.

Wrought Iron Works

We also offer specialist services in the creation of bespoke wrought iron works, blending traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to produce pieces of exceptional quality and character. From decorative gates and railings to functional architectural features, each project is tailored to suit the style and requirements of our clients. Whether for domestic settings or larger commercial applications, our wrought iron work combines strength, durability, and aesthetic appeal. With meticulous attention to detail, we create designs that are not only practical but also enhance the beauty and individuality of any property.

Pipe Bending Services and Equipment

Our workshop provides expert pipe bending services, supported by our role as an authorised distributor of Ercolina pipe bending machines. Equipped to bend hollow sections, pipes, and solid bars, we offer both precise angle bends and rolling for larger, sweeping curves. This versatility allows us to handle projects of all sizes, from structural components to intricate architectural details. We are also fully equipped to bend railings for spiral staircases, ensuring a perfect fit and flawless finish. Combining advanced machinery with skilled operation, we deliver accurate, high-quality bends to meet the exact specifications of every project.
